How it works

Leave a request

Leave a request on the website or send a DM on Instagram.

Discuss the task

You will be advised on your art and an idea will be brought up for your design for free.

Deposit

After the full deposit invoice is cleared, your design will be worked on within the fixed deadline.

Sketch & free fix

The draft will been drawn and you will be shown the intermediate result. Make free corrections at the sketch stage.

Final stage

Once the artwork is finalized with revisions you will be mailed the final version of the artwork.
